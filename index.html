Reinforcement Sequence

  1. Identify the exact project type and measurements before estimating.
  2. Separate material quantity, prep, delivery, labor, and special conditions.
  3. Use the references to compare assumptions before requesting a quote or ordering material.
  4. Confirm final requirements with local suppliers, contractors, inspectors, or engineers when applicable.
